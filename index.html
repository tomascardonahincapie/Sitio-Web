My name is Lars Olson, and I have a passion for crafting beautiful and intuitive experiences with purpose. I specialize in UI/UX design and game development, often doing both at the same time. With 10 years of experience doing so, I love to push the boundaries of creative interface design while maintaining clear usability with accessible practices for all.

My cumulative knowledge of different fields of design is integrated into everything I do. I'm often inspired by tactile and mechanical interfaces of the mid-20th century.

You'll find me working across the entire UI/UX pipeline — from wireframing and prototyping to visual concepting and technical implementation. As a wearer of many hats, you might also find me coding my own small games, nerding out about typography, making art in the third dimension, capturing daily moments on a film camera, or making thousands of 💯 styled emoji. I'm always curious and always learning...

I'm a native Wisconsinite and certified lover of pineapple on pizza. I've created two small independent game companies in the past. I currently reside in Austin, Texas.

My mission as a designer and game developer is to combine creative mechanics, aesthetics, and a whole lot of polish — all in the name of crafting experiences that spark joy.

I approach everything I do with curiosity and a collaborative spirit.
